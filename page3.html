Color Guard

Description: The Fall Color Guard is an extension of the Penn Marching Kingsmen that serves to support the ensemble visually with various pieces of equipment and through dance. The Fall Color Guard performs along with the Penn Marching Band at home football games and marching band contests. We are highly competitive and have placed in statewide competitions. 

Eligibility Requirements: Color guard members must pass an audition.

Sponsor/Coach Name: Jen Buckholz

E-Mail: tenny1929@yahoo.com 

Meetings: 18 hours per week / 4 to 5 times per week after school. Marching band (fall color guard) also practices during the summer. 

Where: Fine Arts area

Membership Limit: none

Procedure for Joining: Audition

Winter Guard

Description: The Winter Guard is a continuation and expansion of the Fall Color Guard. The Winter Guard program currently includes five different level-based ensembles, all of which compete in contests throughout the winter season. Performers focus on refining dance and equipment skills as they prepare their competitive shows. 

Eligibility Requirements: Color guard members must pass an audition.

Sponsor/Coach Name: Jen Buckholz

E-Mail: tenny1929@yahoo.com 

Meetings: 18 hours per week / 4 to 5 times per week after school. Marching band (fall color guard) also practices during the summer. 

Where: Fine Arts area

Membership Limit: none
